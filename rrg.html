Rules

Submit a paper to attend (there is no representation without taxation). Regular attendance is expected. Organizers select 0-2 papers for 20-min presentations (with slides). Non-selected submitters may be called for 5-min presentations (no slides). All participants must be prepared to discuss all papers.

Schedule

Members submit their papers by Friday 5pm ahead of each Wednesday meeting. 20-min presenters will be notified Friday evening. Meetings take place on Wednesday at 5pm (Washington DC time) via Microsoft Teams.
