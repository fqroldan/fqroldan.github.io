Rules

Submit a paper to attend. No representation without taxation. Regular attendance is expected. Organizers select 0-3 papers for 20-min presentations (with slides). Non-selected submitters may be called for 5-min presentations (no slides). All participants must be prepared to discuss all papers.

Schedule

Members submit their papers by Friday 5pm for the following Wednesday meeting. Meetings take place on Wednesday at 5pm (Washington DC time) via Microsoft Teams.
